\ \ Installation Guide\ +\ \
\
    \
  1. Excavate pond to desired shape, adding 2" depth for underlayment
    2. \
  2. Remove sharp rocks and roots; smooth soil with hand tamper
    3. \
  3. Install protective underlayment (sold separately)
    4. \
  4. Unfold liner in sun for 30 minutes to increase flexibility
    5. \
  5. Drape liner into pond, pleating at corners
    6. \
  6. Fill gradually, adjusting folds as water settles liner
    7. \
  7. Trim excess, leaving 12" overlap around edges
    8. \
  8. Secure with rocks, pavers, or edging material
